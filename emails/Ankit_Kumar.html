Hi Ankit,

Your HW-CNN and Wasserstein work on plant disease detection fights a problem I think about a lot — getting a model to lock onto the disease signature instead of confounding background that only correlates. That correlation-vs-cause gap is exactly what we chase in biology.

We built BiRAGAS: it takes raw sequencing data down to a few causal disease drivers, with the data lineage behind each edge kept explicit. On a public AML cohort it called 8 of 11 drug responses right and abstained rather than bluff on the rest.
