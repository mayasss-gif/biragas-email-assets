Hi Chitra,

Your zebrafish epilepsy work — threading GEO differential expression and network pharmacology into a validated HDAC/c-FOS target set — sits right where the hard part is: telling true causal drivers from co-expressed correlates.

That's what we built BiRAGAS to formalize: one chain from FASTQ through DEG and enrichment to a causal DAG, fusing multiple evidence streams into a short list of causal drivers — each edge scored for causal confidence and carrying its lineage. On BeatAML2 it retroactively called 8 of 11 AML drug responses right, and abstained rather than bluff on the rest.

We built a 51-question Help Companion that walks through how it works — worth a look? https://mayasss-gif.github.io/BiRAGAS-Help-Companion/
